What are Beach-cleanups..?

Beach cleanups are organized events where volunteers gather to remove trash and litter from beaches and coastal areas. Participants are provided with gloves and bags to collect garbage like plastic bottles, wrappers, and debris. The goal is to improve the cleanliness of the beach, protect marine life, and raise awareness about pollution. Collected trash is disposed of properly, and data may be collected to track pollution levels. Beach cleanups are vital for preserving the environment and engaging communities in environmental stewardship.
